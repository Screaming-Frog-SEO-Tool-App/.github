## 🚀 At a Glance
Screaming Frog for Windows is a desktop crawler that gives you a reliable, repeatable way to audit a website's SEO health. As a focused screaming frog SEO tool, it fetches your pages, records their status codes and on-page attributes, and highlights the problems that quietly hold sites back — broken links, thin metadata, redirect chains, and duplicate content. The result is a clear worklist instead of a vague hunch. SEO specialists, developers, and marketing teams rely on it for accurate technical audits, and it handles everything from small brochure sites to large, content-heavy platforms.

## 🌟 What You Get
| Feature | What it does for you |

## ❓ FAQ
**Is Screaming Frog free to use?**
There is a free tier for smaller crawls, plus an optional paid license that unlocks larger crawls and advanced configuration. You choose the level that fits your work.

**Which versions of Windows work?**
64-bit Windows 10 and Windows 11 are supported. A 64-bit system is required, and more memory helps when crawling large sites.

**Screaming Frog vs Semrush — how do they differ?**
Semrush is an all-in-one cloud marketing suite covering keywords, backlinks, and reporting, while Screaming Frog is a desktop crawler specialized in deep technical and on-page audits. They work well together: use Screaming Frog to inspect a site closely and a platform like Semrush for broader research and monitoring.

**Where is my data stored?**
On your own Windows PC. Crawl results stay local, so your audit data never has to leave your machine unless you export it.
